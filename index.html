08 · Important Limitations

Authentic Image Inspector by JLV provides a probabilistic estimation based on technical and visual indices. A compressed, cropped, edited, or screenshot-captured image can significantly reduce analysis reliability. The absence of metadata is not proof of AI generation. Only reliable provenance signatures — such as certain C2PA or Content Credentials mechanisms — can provide stronger evidence of origin or modification.

This tool does not perform facial recognition and must not be used to identify individuals.
